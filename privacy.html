Privacy Policy for MathQuatro

Last updated: April 5, 2026

MathQuatro (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and shared when you use the MathQuatro app.

MathQuatro is an educational math app designed to provide a fun and interactive way for children and beginner learners to practice arithmetic skills.

1. Information We May Collect

We do not require users to create an account to use the core features of MathQuatro. However, the app and its integrated third-party services may collect certain categories of data, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • Product interaction or usage data
  • Advertising data
  • Crash data
  • Performance data
  • Diagnostic information

This information may be collected automatically through the app or through third-party services that help us operate, improve, and support MathQuatro.

2. How We Use Information

We may use collected information for purposes such as:

  • Providing and maintaining app functionality
  • Fixing bugs, crashes, and technical issues
  • Improving app performance and stability
  • Understanding how users interact with the app
  • Supporting analytics and service improvement
  • Displaying and improving advertising content
  • Supporting marketing and advertising measurement

3. Advertising and Tracking

MathQuatro may display advertisements through third-party advertising partners, including Google AdMob.

The app may request permission through Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. If permission is granted, certain data may be used for personalized advertising, ad measurement, and related tracking purposes, in accordance with Apple’s policies and your device settings.

If permission is not granted, advertising behavior may be more limited or less personalized, depending on the platform, region, and advertising configuration.

Important: You can manage tracking and advertising permissions in your iPhone or iPad settings at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

MathQuatro uses third-party services to support app functionality, diagnostics, and advertising. These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

Examples of third-party services used by MathQuatro may include:

  • Google AdMob for advertising services
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting and diagnostics

These third-party services may process information such as device information, identifiers, advertising-related data, interaction data, crash logs, and performance data.

5. Children’s Privacy

MathQuatro is designed as an educational app for children and beginner learners. We do not knowingly require children to create accounts or submit personal profile information in order to use the app’s core learning features.

Because the app may use third-party advertising and diagnostic services, some technical information may still be processed automatically as part of app operation, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising delivery.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information directly to third parties.

We may share or allow access to certain data with service providers and technology partners only as necessary to:

  • Operate the app
  • Analyze app performance
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Serve advertisements
  • Measure advertising effectiveness

7. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and improve app performance and reliability.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Choices

You may have choices regarding how certain information is used, including:

  • Managing tracking permissions through Apple’s privacy settings
  • Limiting ad tracking features through device settings
  • Stopping use of the app at any time

If you would like to contact us regarding privacy questions, please use the contact information below.
